About Kristi

I've been through the dissertation process myself and I know how isolating the final stretch can be — when feedback piles up, motivation stalls, and the finish line keeps moving. My job is to help you work through that.

I hold a PhD in Educational Technology and Instructional Design and two master's degrees. I've spent over twenty years teaching English and writing at levels ranging from middle school to college, and I've led workshops on thesis writing and essay construction. I bring that same care for clear thinking and strong writing to every project I take on.

My approach is practical and low-pressure. We figure out where you are, what you need, and how to move forward.
