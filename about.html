About Kristi

Dissertation editor, academic coach, and educator with more than twenty years of experience helping students write with clarity and confidence.

I've been through the dissertation process myself, and I know how isolating the final stretch can feel — when feedback piles up, motivation slows, and the finish line seems to keep moving. Many students reach this stage feeling overwhelmed or unsure how to move forward. My role is to help you regain clarity and momentum.

I hold a PhD in Educational Technology and Instructional Design along with two master's degrees, and I've spent more than twenty years teaching English and writing at levels ranging from middle school through college. Throughout my career, I've guided students through complex writing projects, thesis development, and academic revision — work that requires both strong writing skills and thoughtful mentorship.

My approach is practical, supportive, and low-pressure. We start by understanding where you are in the process, identify what's creating friction, and create a clear path forward so you can continue progressing with confidence.

Students often come to Dissertation Unstuck when they feel stuck between drafts, uncertain how to respond to committee feedback, or preparing for an important milestone like proposal or defense. Together, we focus on clarity, structure, and steady progress — without judgment or overwhelm.
