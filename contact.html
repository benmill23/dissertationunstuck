Book a Consultation

Taking the first step doesn't require everything to be figured out.

Share a little about where you are in your dissertation or thesis process and what feels most challenging right now. Kristi personally reviews every inquiry and will follow up to schedule a conversation where you can talk through your goals, timeline, and the type of support that would be most helpful.

There's no commitment — just a chance to gain clarity on your next steps.

What to include

You can share as much or as little detail as you'd like, but helpful information may include:

  • Your program or field of study
  • Where you are in the dissertation or thesis process
  • Any challenges or feedback you're currently navigating
  • Approximate timeline (if known)

It's completely okay if you're unsure — that's common.
