The Process

How It Works

No commitment upfront. No complicated process. Just a clear path from where you are to where you need to be.

1

Start the Conversation

Book a consultation and share a little about where you are in your dissertation or thesis process. You don't need everything figured out. Just a general sense of what you're working on and where you feel stuck.

2

Talk It Through

I personally review every inquiry and meet with you to understand your goals, challenges, and timeline. Together, we'll discuss the type of support that would be most helpful and review scope and pricing clearly.

3

Begin Working Together

Once you're comfortable moving forward, editing or coaching begins based on the agreed scope. You'll receive structured feedback, guidance, and support tailored to your specific stage of the process.

4

Move Toward Completion

Refine your work, respond to feedback with confidence, and prepare for submission or defense with a clear plan forward.
