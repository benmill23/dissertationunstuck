Dissertation Support

I commonly help students with:

  • Clarifying research problems and research questions
  • Strengthening structure and argument flow
  • Interpreting committee feedback
  • Revising literature reviews for synthesis and coherence
  • Organizing qualitative or mixed-methods findings
  • Preparing for proposal or final defense
