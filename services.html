Services

Three Ways to Work Together

Every student reaches a different point in the dissertation process. These services can stand alone or be combined depending on the kind of support you need.

Dissertation & Thesis Editing

For students who have written most of their dissertation or thesis but want expert guidance to strengthen clarity, structure, and academic flow before submission.

Full draft or chapter-by-chapter editing
Argument clarity and flow refinement
Pre-committee submission review
Feedback designed to strengthen your voice, not replace it

References & Formatting

For students who want confidence that citations, formatting, and references meet academic standards before submission.

APA and MLA formatting review
Citation and reference cleanup
Identification of missing or inconsistent citations
Document consistency check

Mentoring & Defense Preparation

For students who feel stuck, overwhelmed by revisions, or unsure how to respond to committee feedback.

Defense preparation support
Guidance interpreting committee feedback
Revision planning and prioritization
Accountability and structured progress

Not sure which service fits?

Reach out with your project details and I will help you figure out the right approach. There is no commitment. We can meet to discuss your work and see whether I can help.
